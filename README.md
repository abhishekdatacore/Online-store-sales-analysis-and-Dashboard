# Online-store-sales-analysis-and-Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes over 30,000 sales records from an online store to uncover revenue trends, customer demographics, and top-performing sales channels.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights using Excel-based data analysis and visualization techniques.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The company aims to:

Identify revenue trends across months and regions

Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior

Determine top-performing sales channels

Identify high-revenue states and customer segments

Enable data-driven decision-making through interactive reporting

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Pivot Charts

Slicers

Data Cleaning

Data Filtering & Sorting

Dashboard Design

## 🔍 Key Insights

Revenue is concentrated in specific states and regions.

Certain sales channels significantly outperform others.

A specific customer segment contributes disproportionately to revenue.

Seasonal patterns impact sales performance.

## 💼 Business Impact

This analysis helps the business to:

Optimize marketing strategies by focusing on high-performing channels

Allocate resources to high-revenue regions

Target profitable customer segments

Improve strategic planning using data-driven insights
